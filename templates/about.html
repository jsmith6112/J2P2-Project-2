ABOUT US

The SBA serves as the nation's only go-to resource and voice for small businesses. It provides tools and resources critical to the success of America's nearly 30 million small businesses, which support more than 56 million jobs. It is the only cabinet-level federal agency fully dedicated to small businesses and provides services which include:

  • Counseling
  • Capital
  • Contracting
Our project focuses on loans from the 7a program. It the most common loan program and is used to help business throughout the life of the business. Funds are used to start, acquire, operate, and expand. In 2019, The SBA approved $23B to small businesses in the US
